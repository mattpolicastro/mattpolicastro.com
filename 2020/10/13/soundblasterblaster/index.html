SoundBlasterBlaster

2020-10-13

Well I left the team at Clearhead (more on that to come soon), but as I was wrapping things up I wanted to build a quick little thing to celebrate all the shenanigans I got up to with virtual soundboards in my tenure. So, meet SoundBlasterBlaster.

It's decidedly silly but it's the first thing I've been able to push in a long, long time and I actually think it's not all that badly-built? A simple Vue.js app with some audio clips from yours truly. Configuring and customizing it is probably a little more painful than it could be, so we'll see if I keep tinkering with it.
